GIS Portfolio


Above are two sample factsheet created during my time at the Institution of Immigant Research. The data is collected from IPUMS Census data, cleaned and converted into charts and table. With the data, I was also able to create maps and visualize the immigrant data over the population to show the demographics and characteristics of the population.










This was one of my first ever web maps I had created. This web application shows the National Heritage Sites around the world and gives details of each location if you were to click on the pin. I used leaflet styling for this which is why it has a simple layout




This map is of New York City's Green Taxi pick up points. It might be a little laggy to load since it has many functionalities as well as is a dynamic map. I added a timeline to show the location and time of where the pickup points were in the boroughs outside of Manhattan by the hour. There are cluster layers which will change depending on the zoom level.




Above is a map I created on ArcGIS Online. I gathered the shapefile data from GMU's GIS data library and cleaned up the shapefiles as well as added building polygons that were not on the original shapefiles. I put it all together on an online map for everyone to view. This web map is intended to be a interactive map for students and other users to see the buildings around campus.
